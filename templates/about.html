About Bret Taylor

I am currently Director of Products at Facebook, where I work on the Facebook Platform. Previously, I was the co-founder and CEO of FriendFeed. Facebook acquired FriendFeed in August 2009. If you want to know more about me, subscribe to my FriendFeed or visit my Facebook profile.

Before starting FriendFeed, I was a Group Product Manager at Google, where I co-created Google Maps and Google Local. After Google Maps, I helped start Google's developer product group.

You can reach me at btaylor@gmail.com, though I am notoriously bad at answering email.
